Begin Date 
Begin Date is a date used throughout the model to reflect the best known date
on which the related data was recorded or changed.  It may be the date of change
notification, the date of change was entered to a system (preferably the system
of record), or the date that the related database was updated.  The date that is
used should be consistent throughout the implemented solution.
